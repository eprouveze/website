## Software Benefits (Explored)

User feedback: "Software is not just about saving time for people or being more efficient."

### Agency (Refined)
AI and low-code inside enterprise platforms empower business users to solve their own problems—not shadow IT, but sanctioned capability.

> A business user spots a gap in their process. Five years ago, they'd submit a request to IT and wait 18 months. Today, they describe what they need, and AI-assisted tools help them build it—within governed platforms, with proper guardrails. They're not going around the system. They're using the system the way it was meant to be used.

**The deeper point**: Software used to require specialists. Now it amplifies what non-specialists can do. That's agency.

### Enabling What Wasn't Possible (Refined)
Data-driven decisions based on patterns humans couldn't see—not because humans aren't smart, but because the volume is inhuman.

> A thousand signals across a thousand accounts. No human can hold that in their head. But software can surface the pattern—the risk you didn't see, the opportunity hiding in the noise. You still make the call. But now you're deciding with sight, not just intuition.

**The deeper point**: Software doesn't just improve the old way. It creates new ways that weren't on the menu before.
