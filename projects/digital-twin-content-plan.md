### 1.2 10 Prompts to Test Your AI's Voice

**URL**: `/resources/ai-prompts`
**Target**: ChatGPT/Claude power users
**Format**: 3-4 page PDF with copy-paste prompts

**Content Outline**:

```
PAGE 1: Introduction
- "These 10 prompts expose whether AI truly sounds like you"
- How to use: Run each prompt, compare output to your actual writing
- Scoring: Rate each 1-5, calculate total

PAGE 2-3: The Prompts

PROMPT 1: Formal Email to Leadership
"Write an email to the CEO updating them on [project X] status.
Include one concern and one win. Keep it under 150 words."
→ Compare: Does it match your executive communication style?

PROMPT 2: Casual Slack to Team
"Write a Slack message asking the team to review a document
before EOD. Add a touch of personality."
→ Compare: Would your team recognize this as you?

PROMPT 3: Declining a Meeting
"Write an email declining a meeting invite politely but firmly.
I have a conflict and can't reschedule."
→ Compare: Too apologetic? Too blunt? Just right?

PROMPT 4: Explaining Technical Concept
"Explain [technical concept] to a non-technical stakeholder
in 3 sentences."
→ Compare: Your simplification style?

PROMPT 5: Giving Critical Feedback
"Write feedback on a report that needs significant revision.
Be constructive but clear about issues."
→ Compare: Your feedback delivery style?

PROMPT 6: Quick Thank You
"Write a brief thank-you message to someone who helped with
a tight deadline."
→ Compare: Your gratitude expression style?

PROMPT 7: Meeting Follow-Up
"Write a follow-up email after a client meeting summarizing
3 action items and next steps."
→ Compare: Your summarization style?

PROMPT 8: Announcing a Decision
"Write an announcement that we're changing our project
approach due to new information."
→ Compare: How you frame changes?

PROMPT 9: Requesting Help
"Write a message asking a colleague for help with something
outside their normal responsibilities."
→ Compare: How you ask for favors?

PROMPT 10: Brainstorm Response
"Respond to a brainstorm thread with one idea and one concern
about another idea mentioned."
→ Compare: Your collaborative style?

PAGE 4: Scoring & Analysis

- 10-24: Generic AI voice, not yours
```

## 3. Blog Post Template

```markdown
# [Title with Primary Keyword]

**Meta description**: [150-160 chars with keyword]
**Target keyword**: [primary keyword]
**Secondary keywords**: [2-3 related terms]
**Word count target**: [X words]
**CTA**: [Lead magnet or signup]

---

## Hook (100-150 words)
- Start with relatable problem or surprising stat
- Establish credibility (personal experience)
- Promise what reader will learn

## The Problem (300-400 words)
- Expand on the pain point
- Use specific examples
- Include data if available
- Make reader feel understood

## Why Common Solutions Fail (200-300 words)
- Address what they've already tried
- Explain why it doesn't work
- Build toward your solution

## The Solution (500-800 words)
- Core methodology or approach
- Step-by-step if applicable
- Examples and specifics
- Screenshots or visuals

## Results / Social Proof (200-300 words)
- Before/after comparison
- Time saved or outcomes
- Testimonials if available

## Call to Action (100-150 words)
- Relevant lead magnet OR
- Free account signup OR
- Product pitch (soft)

---

**Internal links**: [Related posts]
**External links**: [Authoritative sources]
```
