What Your Score Means

If you scored 40-50: You've likely invested time in custom instructions or have a naturally AI-friendly writing style. Focus on fine-tuning specific contexts where AI still misses.

If you scored 25-39: AI captures some aspects of your voice but misses others. Creating structured voice documentation—covering tone, formality levels, and context-specific adjustments—would significantly improve output quality.

If you scored 10-24: There's a major gap between how you write and how AI writes for you. Generic prompts won't fix this. You need a comprehensive voice profile that captures your unique patterns, preferences, and contextual variations.
