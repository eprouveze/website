What These Results Tell You

Look for patterns: Did AI struggle more with casual messages or formal ones? Feedback or requests? The prompts where you scored lowest reveal which aspects of your voice need the most work.

Context matters: If AI handles some situations well but fails in others, you need context-specific voice rules—not just general instructions.

The editing test: For each prompt, ask yourself: "How long would it take me to edit this to actually sound like me?" If the answer is consistently "longer than writing it myself," your AI isn't saving you time.
